Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 28, 2025 • Contact: lockdhelp@gmail.com

LOCKD is a discipline and consistency app for iOS that helps users build habits, track distractions, block distracting apps, and maintain daily consistency. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By using LOCKD, you agree to this Policy.

1) Who we are

“LOCKD” (© 2025 LOCKD by Zane Tuning) operates the LOCKD iOS app. Contact: lockdhelp@gmail.com.

2) What we collect

Profile & Account

  • User ID (UUID), email (if signed in), first name, age
  • Motivation (“why”), non-negotiable habits, payment/subscription status, streak data

Tasks, Habits, Goals

  • Daily tasks, completion status, priorities, due dates, notes
  • Habits & completion history, goals & progress

Focus & Activity

  • Focus session durations, schedules, break timers
  • Blocked app selections (bundle identifiers) via Apple Screen Time / Family Controls

Onboarding Responses

  • Focus areas, distractions, screen time estimates, routine, notification preferences

Device & Local Cache

  • Stored locally using iOS UserDefaults/App Groups: blocked bundles, schedules, preferences

3) How we use data

  • Provide and improve app features (habits, tasks, focus tracking, app blocking)
  • Sync your data across devices when signed in (via Supabase)
  • Manage subscriptions and paywalls (via SuperwallKit / Apple IAP)
  • Send optional notifications (reminders, streaks, summaries)
  • Security, debugging, and preventing abuse

4) Third-party services

Supabase (Backend/DB) — user auth, storage, sync • HTTPS/TLS
Apple Services — Screen Time / Family Controls, Device Activity, Sign in with Apple, In-App Purchases, Notifications
SuperwallKit — paywalls & subscription status (Apple handles payments)

5) Legal bases (GDPR)

  • Contract: to provide the app/features you request
  • Legitimate interests: app security, debugging, product improvement
  • Consent: notifications; optional sign-in; certain processing where required

6) Data sharing

We share data with service providers listed above only to operate the app. We do not sell personal data. Payments are processed by Apple.

7) Data retention

We retain data while your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete associated Supabase records within a reasonable period. Local caches can be cleared from the app.

8) Security

We use HTTPS/TLS and rely on Apple’s secure storage mechanisms (Keychain, App Groups) where appropriate. No system is 100% secure.

9) International transfers

Supabase and Apple may process data on servers outside your region. We rely on standard safeguards and provider compliance programs.

10) Children

LOCKD is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child provided data, contact us for deletion.

11) Your rights

  • Access, correct, delete your data
  • Export data (JSON/CSV) from the app
  • Object or restrict certain processing (where applicable)
  • CCPA: right to know, delete, and non-discrimination

Request via lockdhelp@gmail.com. We may need to verify your identity.

12) Cookies & tracking

No third-party ads or trackers. Standard platform logs and secure sessions only.

13) Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy. We’ll revise the “Effective date” and may notify you in-app for significant changes.
