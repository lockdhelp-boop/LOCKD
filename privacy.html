Privacy Policy

LOCKD values your privacy. We do not collect, sell, or share personal data beyond what is necessary to provide app functionality.

The app uses Apple’s FamilyControls and DeviceActivity APIs to block or allow apps based on your choices. All data related to your focus sessions and app selections remain on your device or securely synced via your iCloud or Supabase account.

We do not track your activity outside of LOCKD, and we never share data with third parties.

For support or questions, contact lockdhelp@gmail.com.
